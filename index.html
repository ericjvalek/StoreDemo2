About Uncle McGee

Reginald Alfred Willaim Perryman "Uncle" Mcgee III was borin in 1946 in a shack in rural West Virgina. Locals say that his appearance as a grizzled old man took hold at the age of nine, and some believe him to be immortal.

He entered the business of peddling trinkets in the late sixties, when he would open shop at ports and haggle prices on random scraps he found along the docks to incoming sailors. He returnd to his West Virgina shack in 1980, and has since resumed his business there.

Uncle McGee is also an avid jug blower, and has released forty-six albums. His most recent, "The Call of the Moonshine", has gone triple-platinum.
